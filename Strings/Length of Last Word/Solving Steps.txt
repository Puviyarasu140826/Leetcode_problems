Length of Last Word is the 58th problem on leetcode.

Problem Statement:
      Given a string s consisting of words and spaces, return the length of the last word in the string.A word is a maximal substring consisting of non-space characters only.

Steps to solve:
  1. Iterate the loop by backwards.
  
  2. Check for blank space from last.
  
  3. Until getting blank space increment the count variable for every charcters.
  
  4. Return the count after getting blank space from last.
